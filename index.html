About Me

I’m Heo, a developer and AI enthusiast from the Dominican Republic pursuing Computer Science and Mathematics. My journey into tech started as a kid in a small cyber café run by my aunt, where I grew up surrounded by computers, printers, photocopiers, and old video games handed down by my cousins.

What began as helping troubleshoot hardware and spending hours exploring the ins and outs of my PS2, Wii, and NDS became a deeper curiosity about how technology works beneath the surface. Today, I build software and AI-driven solutions with the same mindset that started years ago: understand systems deeply, solve meaningful problems, and create things that people genuinely enjoy using.
